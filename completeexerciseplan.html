So at the end of the daily workout session, we would like you to do the shavasana. This helps in relaxation and just letting go at the end of the day.

Fun Fact: It is also referred to as the Corpse Pose!

Here are the steps:

1. Lie flat on the floor, ensuring that there will be no disturbance for the duration of the pose. Make sure you are comfortable, but donâ€™t use any pillows or cushions. It will be best if you lie on a hard surface.

2. Just close your eyes

3. Place your legs shoulder width apart. Make sure your legs are completely relaxed and your toes are facing sideways.

4. Make sure your arms are placed along your body with your palms open and facing upwards

5. Breathe slowly and draw attention to every part of your body. Remember not to fall asleep in the process!!

6. Focus on yourself and your body, forgetting all other tasks. This might be the time when people fall asleep and it's not recommended to fall asleep, but if you do it's okay.

7. Keep on breathing in and out deeply but slowly.

8. Stay in that pose for 10 - 12 minutes

9. After that, roll to one side (if you are right handed, roll to your right side and if you are left handed, roll to the left side)

10. Stay in that position for a minute and then sit up

11. Take a few deep breaths and open your eyes
