'Don't talk to strangers!'

Most of us duck our heads and stare at our phones when faced with strangers. Whether it's in a train, bus, or uber, most people don't interact with others around them. They're worried that the stranger might be mean, dangerous or just not want to talk, and while those concerns may be legitimate, it's been shown that chatting with strangers makes you happier and improves your day!

However, finding something to talk about can be challenging. This page is here to help you. Below are some jokes and topics to bring up!
